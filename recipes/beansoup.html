Bean Soup recipe

Here’s a cozy bowl that’s so hearty and satisfying, you’ll never want to put your spoon down.
Try this hearty and easy bean soup! Make room in your heart for this recipe, because it dazzles.
Kidney beans and navy beans float in a savory, smoky broth of fire roasted tomatoes that has just
the right glossy texture. It uses canned beans, but it tastes like it’s been simmering all day.
Each silky, smoky spoonful is a bit of heaven. (Plus, it saves well too!)

Ingredients

  • beans - 1.5 lb (700 gr)
  • leeks - 1 smal stalk
  • onion - 1 onion
  • oil - 2/5 cup (100ml)
  • paprika - 1 tbsp.
  • salt - by taste
  • black pepper - by taste
  • bay leaf - 3 pcs.
  • garlic - 4 cloves
  • baking soda - 1/2 tbsp.

Steps

  1. Soak the beans in water, so that they can swell overnight.
  2. Pour out the water the next day, add fresh water and boil the beans.
  3. Add salt, baking soda, bay leaf and boil, until they're fully cooked.
  4. Fry the onions, leeks and garlic. When they soften, add the allspice
  5. Pour everything into the water with beans and add black pepper and baking soda.
  6. Boil the bean soup for another 30 minutes.
  7. The Serbian-Style Bean Soup is ready.
